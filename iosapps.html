SKIP

The lines for major attractions can have long wait times. Skip-the-line tickets are not providing an optimal solution. The native IOS app SKIP allows a client to pay another client ahead of them in line to get out of the line, with the client originally behind them taking their spot. The clients can communicate with each other and decide on a price. Thus, SKIP is an app that that works as a medium and transactional service for lines. This app is not published to the AppStore and was just a prototype built by me and a partner at a hackathon.
